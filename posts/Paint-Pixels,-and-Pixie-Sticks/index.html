Paint Pixels, and Pixie Sticks

Posted Feb 2, 2023
By Amanda Mullins
1 min read

Overview:

In this assignmentwe had to choose a painting made before 1850 with a wide range of colors. Using photoshop to then split the image into a 8in square area, which will be used to do a color study on the image. Then recreating that area in pixels with the color-aid paper. Going back to the orignal image, taking the warm and cool colors we create a color wheel, and then finally recreating the whole orignal peice on a 16x20 piece of paper.

Progress:

The artwork I choose was “Watson and the Shark” by John Singleton Copley.
